# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in AiDex a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Be excellent to each other:**
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community

**Not acceptable:**
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub Issues, Discussions, Pull Requests, and any other community channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to `u.chalas@csc-software.de`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.
